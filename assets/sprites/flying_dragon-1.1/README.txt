Name:
  Flying Dragon Rework
Version:
  1.1
Description:
  ZaPaper's Red Dragon reworked as drop-in replacement for Stendhal.
Details:
  - Orientation: orthogonal
    - N/E/S/W format for Stendhal.
    - I plan to create S/W/E/N versions for RPG Maker & RPGBoss.
  - Dimensions: 144x128 & 191x161
  - Animation:
    - flying: 12 frames (3 per direction)
    - Animated preview @ 150ms delay.
  - PNG images with 256 colors or less use indexed color (smaller files).
  - Images with more than 256 colors & GIMP sources (.xcf) use RGB color.
