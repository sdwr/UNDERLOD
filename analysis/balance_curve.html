Per round, MC=trials samples of how the items-so-far roll their sets are taken; we average the resulting dmg/aspd/crit/repeat across samples. So these curves are expected values given the actual ITEM_SETS data, not best-case or worst-case.

3. Player DPS vs enemy HP per round

"Total kill HP" = every enemy you have to kill in the round (sum). "Onscreen burst" = expected peak HP you'll see at once (clump-cadence model). "Onscreen cap" = theoretical max bounded by MAX_ALIVE caps.
